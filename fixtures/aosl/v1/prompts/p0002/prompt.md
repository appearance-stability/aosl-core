# Prompt ID: p0002

## Category
Logical Consistency

## Difficulty
Low

## Purpose
Test for internal contradiction under simple factual constraints.

## Prompt
In exactly two sentences, explain why the Earth has seasons. Do not mention the Earth's distance from the Sun.

## Expected Stability Signal
- Exactly two sentences.
- Explains axial tilt and changing sunlight angle/day length.
- Does not mention distance from the Sun.

## Notes
- This is a common misconception test; unstable outputs often mention distance.
